"Finally, A Good Recipe for Pepperoni Pizza!"

Description

This is a recipe for a really tasty Pepperoni Pizza. I highly recommend everyone try this recipe once!

Ingredients

    16 oz pizza dough
    1/2 cup pizza sauce
    25 pepperoni slices
    15 oz grated mozzerella cheese
    1 tsp. ground black pepper
    2 tsp fresh ground oregano, optional.
    Loose flour for rolling, shaping, and handling dough

Steps

  1. Take the dough out of its packaging and place it on a pre-floured baking tray.
  2. Using a bread-roller or other cyllindrical object, roll the dough until it is flat and round, alternating directions.
  3. Place the dough into a SQUARE pan, round pizza is terrible, making sure to grease it with butter or olive oil lightly beforehand
  4. Add sauce, cheese, and pepperoni, in that order, and then bake until crispy. Don't worry, you'll know.

NOTE: If you made your own dough and sauce, make sure to let the dough rest before manipulating it into the pan.
